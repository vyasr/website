Vyas Ramasubramani is a software engineer at NVIDIA on the
[RAPIDS](https://github.com/rapidsai/) team, where he develops
GPU-accelerated libraries for data science and machine learning. He
primarily works on cuDF, a high-performance GPU DataFrame library. He
holds a Ph.D. in Chemical Engineering and Scientific Computing from the
University of Michigan, where he studied self-assembly in soft matter
physics, and a bachelor's degree in Chemical and Biological Engineering
from Princeton with certificates in Applications of
Computing as well as Quantitative and Computational Biology.
